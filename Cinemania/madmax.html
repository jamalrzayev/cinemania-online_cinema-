rating: 8.1 / 10 year: 2015 director: George Miller

Description of Mad Max

Haunted by the ghosts of a troubled past, Max believes the best way to survive is to wander alone. Despite this, he joins the rebels as they race across the desert in a battle wagon led by Warlord Furiosa. They are trying to escape from the Citadel, suffering from the tyranny of Immortal Joe, from whom they have taken something very valuable. The enraged commander throws all his strength into pursuit of the rebels, stepping on the road of war - the road of fury.
